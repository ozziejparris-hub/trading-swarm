# arXiv 2606.07811 — When Do Markets Fully Process Public Information? Evidence from Real-Time Prediction Markets
## Source
https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07811
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Finds salient public signals are incorporated quickly in liquid prediction markets but produce substantially greater underreaction when liquidity is low, with price discovery shaped by the interaction of attention and trading frictions. Supports targeting low-liquidity/low-attention markets for exploitable post-news mispricing.
