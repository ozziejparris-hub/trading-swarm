# arXiv 2605.02287 — Per-Market Information Leakage and Order-Flow Skill in Decentralised Prediction Markets (May 2026)
## Source

## Domain

## Summary
Introduces two methodological frameworks for detecting informed trading on Polymarket: sign-randomisation tests that classify wallets as skilled or insider, and an Information Leakage Score (ILS) quantifying per-market pre-resolution front-loading. Mitts & Ofir report a 69.9% win rate on trades flagged by the composite insider score across 210,000+ wallet-market pairs.
