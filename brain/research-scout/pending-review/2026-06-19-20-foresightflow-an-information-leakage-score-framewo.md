# ForesightFlow: An Information Leakage Score Framework for Prediction Markets (arXiv 2605.00493)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00493
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Framework for quantifying informed trading on Polymarket: scores what fraction of the terminal information move was priced in before the public news event. Uses Murphy decomposition and three operational preconditions (edge effect, non-trivial total move, anchor sensitivity). Primary application is Polymarket; submitted May 1, 2026 (41 pages, 12 tables).
