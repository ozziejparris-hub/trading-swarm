# When Do Markets Fully Process Public Information? Evidence from Real-Time Prediction Markets (arXiv 2606.07811)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07811
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Uses binary-payoff prediction markets with precisely observed public signals and high-frequency data. Finds prices are informative and accuracy improves near resolution, and prices respond rapidly in the correct direction during events — but directional responsiveness is not the same as efficient updating, leaving exploitable lags.
