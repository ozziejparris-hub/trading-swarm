# The Anatomy of a Decentralized Prediction Market: Microstructure Evidence from the Polymarket Order Book (arXiv 2604.24366)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/html/2604.24366
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Studies Polymarket CLOB order book microstructure directly. Key finding: wider bid-ask spreads attenuate the informational signal surviving to the price — a noisy microstructure produces noisier prices than assumed. Trading cost of holding an informational position is non-trivial.
