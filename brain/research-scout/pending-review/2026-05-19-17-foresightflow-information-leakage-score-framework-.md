# ForesightFlow: Information Leakage Score Framework for Prediction Markets (arXiv 2605.00493)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/html/2605.00493
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Introduces a per-market Information Leakage Score (ILS) to quantify how much early order flow predicts resolution outcomes, providing a tool to identify markets with detectable pre-resolution information asymmetry. Framework is complementary to ELO-based trader ranking and could improve market selection or signal weighting.
