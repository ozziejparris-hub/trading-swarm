# When Do Markets Fully Process Public Information? Evidence from Real-Time Prediction Markets (arXiv 2606.07811)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07811
## Domain
arxiv
## Summary
Uses high-frequency binary prediction market data to examine speed and completeness of price updating on public information arrival. Finds prices improve accuracy near resolution but directional responsiveness does not imply efficient updating.
## Action
Directly relevant to RQ1.1 — the lag between information arrival and efficient updating is the window exploited by ELO-ranked forecasters. Prioritise for RQ design.
