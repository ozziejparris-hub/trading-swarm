## Source

## Summary
May 2026 paper by Mitts and Ofir screening 210,000+ wallet-market pairs on Polymarket. Develops two complementary lenses — per-market information leakage and order-flow skill — to identify informed trading. Finds structurally permissive conditions for trading on material non-public information due to pseudonymous, low-friction, on-chain transparency.
