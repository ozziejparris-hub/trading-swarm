# When Do Markets Fully Process Public Information? Evidence from Real-Time Prediction Markets (arXiv:2606.07811)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07811
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Finds salient public signals are incorporated quickly in liquid prediction markets but generate substantially greater underreaction when liquidity is low, with gradual price discovery shaped by attention and trading frictions. Directly relevant to RQ1.1 and RQ3.2 — liquidity regime may be a confound to control for in both tests.
