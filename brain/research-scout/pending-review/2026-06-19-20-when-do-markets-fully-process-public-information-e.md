# When Do Markets Fully Process Public Information? Evidence from Real-Time Prediction Markets (arXiv 2606.07811)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07811
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Authors Angelini and De Angelis find that a 1-unit move in benchmark probability produces only a 0.64 contemporaneous move in market price; the missing 0.36 predicts future drift over the following several minutes. Underreaction is substantially larger in low-liquidity markets. Uses tick-level real-time prediction market data. Submitted June 5, 2026.
