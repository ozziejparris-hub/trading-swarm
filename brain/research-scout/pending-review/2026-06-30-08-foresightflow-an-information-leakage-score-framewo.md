# ForesightFlow: An Information Leakage Score Framework for Prediction Markets
## Source
https://arxiv.org/html/2605.00493v2
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Proposes a quantitative score for measuring how much private information leaks into prediction market prices ahead of public signals. Screened over 93,000 markets and 210,000 wallet-market pairs, reporting a 69.9% win rate on flagged trades. Directly applicable to identifying informed-trader signals — core to STR-002 and ELO methodology.
