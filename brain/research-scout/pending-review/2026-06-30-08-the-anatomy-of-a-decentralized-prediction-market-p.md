# The Anatomy of a Decentralized Prediction Market: Polymarket Microstructure Study
## Source
https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24366
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Tick-level archive of Polymarket order-book data covering 30 billion events over 52 days. Analyzes trading patterns, liquidity, and market efficiency at microstructure level. Documents that shallow order book depth structurally caps scale of risk-free extraction in both political and sports markets.
