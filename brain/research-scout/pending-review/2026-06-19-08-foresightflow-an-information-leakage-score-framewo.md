# ForesightFlow: An Information Leakage Score Framework for Prediction Markets (arXiv:2605.00493)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/html/2605.00493v2
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Screened 93,000+ markets and 210,000 wallet-market pairs using a composite informed-trader score (cross-sectional bet size, within-trader bet size, profitability, pre-event timing, directional concentration). Flagged trades achieved a 69.9% win rate — replicable methodology for identifying informed order flow on Polymarket.
