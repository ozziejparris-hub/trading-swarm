# arXiv 2606.07811: When Do Markets Fully Process Public Information? Evidence from Real-Time Prediction Markets
## Source
https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07811
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
Angelini & De Angelis (Bologna, June 2026) quantify intraday price discovery on prediction markets. A 1-unit move in the benchmark probability yields only a 0.64-for-one contemporaneous price move; the residual gap predicts future drift over the next several minutes. Underreaction is substantially larger in low-liquidity markets.
