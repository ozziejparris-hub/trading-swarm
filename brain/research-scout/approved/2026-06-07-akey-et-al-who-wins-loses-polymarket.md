# Who Wins and Who Loses In Prediction Markets? Evidence from Polymarket (Akey et al., March 2026)
## Source
https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=6443103
## Domain
ssrn.com / academic paper
## Summary
Comprehensive study of 1.4M Polymarket users, 70M trades, $20B volume (Nov 2022–Oct 2025). Key findings: Top 1% of users capture 84% of all trading gains. Only 1,200 traders (0.05%) took more than half of all profits ($591M, $100K+ each). Gains flow almost entirely to sophisticated traders who outperformed market-implied probabilities. This is the academic paper behind the vgregoire/polymarket-users dataset we have locally.
