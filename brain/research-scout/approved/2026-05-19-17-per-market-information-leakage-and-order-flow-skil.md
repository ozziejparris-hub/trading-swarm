## Summary
New methodology applied to Polymarket classifies 3.14% of accounts as 'skilled winners' and flags 1,950 accounts as likely insiders across 93,000+ markets and 210,000 wallet-market pairs. Composite insider screen reports a 69.9% win rate on flagged trades, providing quantitative evidence of systematic informed trading.
