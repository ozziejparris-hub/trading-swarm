# Prediction Arena: AI Models Lose Money on Live Prediction Markets (arXiv 2604.07355)
## Source
https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07355
## Domain
arxiv.org
## Summary
57-day benchmark (Jan 12–Mar 9 2026) testing 6 frontier AI models in live Polymarket/Kalshi trading with $10,000 each. Final returns: -16% to -30.8% on Kalshi. All models lost money. Key finding: initial prediction accuracy and ability to capitalise on correct predictions are main drivers — not model sophistication. Gemini-3.1-pro-preview executed zero trades on Kalshi but achieved 71.4% settlement win rate on paper trades.
