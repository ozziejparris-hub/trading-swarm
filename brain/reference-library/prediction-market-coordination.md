# Prediction Market Coordination Mechanisms
Source: arXiv:2604.24147
Author: Maksym Nechepurenko

## Core Finding
Prediction market prices function as coordination devices
not pure forecasts. High-visibility markets are LESS 
accurate because they attract coordination-driven trading.

## Signal Credibility Index (SCI)
Composite microstructure metric predicting when price 
movements carry epistemic vs coordination weight.
High SCI = price reflects private information (good)
Low SCI = price reflects coordination/visibility (noise)

## Implications for This System
1. Elite traders who ignore focal-point dynamics and trade
   on private signals have genuine edge — validates ELO premise
2. Market selection: prefer high-SCI markets for signal work
3. Reflexivity warning for Phase 6: front-running elite moves
   at scale becomes a coordination mechanism itself
