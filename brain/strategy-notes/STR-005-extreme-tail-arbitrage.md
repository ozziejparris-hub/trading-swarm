# STR-004 — Extreme Tail Arbitrage (HYPOTHESIS)

## Premise
Markets systematically misprice extreme unlikely outcomes
because retail traders buy YES speculatively on scary
scenarios. This creates inflated YES prices and thus
cheap NO positions on near-impossible outcomes.

## Evidence

exclusively betting NO on crypto extreme downside markets
(BTC to $20K-$45K range). Zero losses. Mechanical, not
predictive.

  "Will nuclear weapon be used in 2025?"
