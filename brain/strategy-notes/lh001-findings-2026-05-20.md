# LH-001 Research Findings — 2026-05-20

## Hypothesis
Single-event geopolitics traders whose first recorded trade occurs within 30 days of
market resolution (lifecycle heuristic) will show higher win rates and profits than
those who entered earlier.

## Methodology
- Phase 1: Identify candidates meeting all 5 criteria (single geo market, resolved,
  volume >= $1K, profit >= $1K, first trade 0-30 days before resolution)
- Phase 2: Compare against control (criteria 1-4, first trade > 30 days before)
- Phase 3: Cross-reference against ELO pool and bot classifications
- Statistical test: Mann-Whitney U (one-tailed, candidates > control)

## Key Findings

**Candidate count: 69** (concentrated on 2 events: Haley 2027, Iran market)

**Statistical result (clean, bots excluded):**
- Mann-Whitney p = 0.0067 (candidates > control)
- Candidate median PnL: $0, control median: -$27,157
- Candidate win rate: 47.7% vs control 43.1%

**Success criteria (2/3 met):**
- ✅ p < 0.05 profit advantage (p=0.0067)
- ✅ 53+ candidates with profit > $5,000
- ❌ Win rate 47.7% (target: 70%)

**Verdict: PARTIALLY VALIDATED**

## Critical Issues Found

1. **integration-contract.md join key is wrong**: condition_id join matches only 63%
   of trades. market_id join is correct (99.999% match). Contract must be corrected.

2. **Iran market title mislabeled**: "Will Iran recognize Iran?" is clearly wrong.
   If this market is test data, 64% of candidates evaporate.

3. **Two distinct phenomena in candidate pool**:
   - Haley candidates: experienced traders (200+ trades, ELO 2300-3316), market specialists
   - Iran candidates: new accounts (10-17 total trades, ELO ~3000 from initial assignment)
   Only the Iran cluster fits the "new account = insider" interpretation from the paper.

4. **Control group bot contamination**: 69.7% known bots without exclusion. The raw
   comparison (including bots) gives p=0.96. Clean comparison requires bot exclusion.

## Limitations
- Only 2 events in dataset — narrow statistical base
- True account creation dates unavailable
- Multiple market_ids per question (YES/NO tokens) inflate apparent single-market count
